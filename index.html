About Me

I started coding when I was 7, starting with Scratch to understand the basics. I was amazed by how I could make a computer do what I want, every command being completed. As I got older I started asking my parents how apps, websites and modules worked - they started showing me coding. I loved it and the thought that I could make anything happen was an outstanding feeling. I started developing prototype websites however I didn't host them as they weren't up to a good standard. I have now developed myself *excuse the pun* into a tech savvy coder and have participated in many coding events including CoderDojo and Young Rewired State Festival Of Code

Where Am I?

Where Am I? was created at Young Rewired State Festival Of Code 2013 with help from Alex Neilsen and Ollie Cole. It is designed to tell you how pratical living in a certain place in Surrey is. It provides information on Fire Incidents, Schools, Average house prices and Crime.
